General Fast Food

What is Fast Food?

Fast Food is a common style of food that can be found all around the world. The food is generally of less quality but are done at an extremely fast time unlike other restaurants who use high quality ingredients.

Fast food can generally be kept at a small price for it is mass produced and cheap to make. Although the food is of bad quality, the food generally tastes good, therefore attracting many people. Likewise, eating too much fast food has been linked to colorectal cancer, obesity and high cholesterol.
